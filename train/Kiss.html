Kiss

by missMARGARITAschuyler

Summary

I kiss her
in a dirty hotel room.
She laughs.

-

I kiss her
goodbye.
No one says anything at all.

I kiss her

in a hotel room.

She laughs.

 

I kiss her

on a Ferris Wheel.

She smiles.

 

I kiss her

in bed. 

She moans.

 

I kiss her

at school.

Someone else calls a slur.

 

I kiss her

in a hallway.

Her father screams.

 

I kiss her

in the ER.

She cries.

 

I kiss her

at her mom’s.

We are congratulated.

 

I kiss her

in a church.

She says, “I do.”

 

I kiss her

in the hospital.

The doctor glares.

 

I kiss her

in a nursery.

Our son coos.

 

I kiss her

on an airplane.

A little girl smiles at us.

 

I kiss her

in a forest.

Our son takes a photo.

 

I kiss her

at a high school.

The teacher smiles.

 

I kiss her

at work.

My boss reprimands me.

 

I kiss her

at a soccer game.

She cheers.

 

I kiss her

in front of our grandchildren.

They whoop.

 

I kiss her

in a nursing home.

The nurse tries to separate us.

 

I kiss her

goodbye.

No one says anything at all.
