To All The Boys I Loved//Reddie AU

by Lyshiab

Summary

Eddie Kaspbrak is invisible. He keeps to himself, does all his school work, and is pretty much under the radar in every possible way. Eddie loves the magic of love, but has never been to able to take that leap of faith to be with someone else after witnessing his parents marriage crumble to pieces. Instead of confessing to his crushes he writes a letter and hides it away. This was a good system for Eddie until his letters were mailed by his little sister to each of his five crushes.

Based on the awesome film: To all the boys I've loved :)
